# Privacy Policy for MapConductor Sample App

**Last Updated: January 14, 2026**

## Overview
MapConductor Sample App is a demonstration application for the MapConductor SDK. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Location Data
- The app may request access to your location to demonstrate map features
- Location data is only used locally on your device to display your position on the map
- We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data to any server
- Location data is never shared with third parties

## Third-Party Services
This app uses the following map providers, each with their own privacy policies:
- Google Maps: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- MapLibre: Open-source, no data collection
- Apple MapKit: [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

Map tiles and data are loaded from these providers when you use the app.

## Data Collection
We do not collect any of the following:
- Personal information
- Usage statistics
- Analytics data
- Crash reports
